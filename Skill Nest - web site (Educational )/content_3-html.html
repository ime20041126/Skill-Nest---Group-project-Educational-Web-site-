Introduction

Digital literacy involves the ability to find, evaluate, use, share, and create content using digital technologies effectively and responsibly.

Benefits of Being Digitally Literate

Digital literacy enhances communication, access to information, job opportunities, and participation in the digital world.

Key Digital Literacy Skills

  • Online communication and collaboration.
  • Understanding how to search and validate information.
  • Basic tech troubleshooting.
  • Using productivity software (like Word, Excel).

Useful Digital Tools

Examples include Google Workspace, Canva, Zoom, Microsoft Office, and online learning platforms like Coursera or Khan Academy.

Practicing Online Safety

Digital literacy also means knowing how to protect your personal information, recognize scams, and use privacy settings effectively.
