Lesson 2: Order of Operations

Master PEMDAS to solve expressions correctly

Order of Operations (PEMDAS)

  • Remember: P → E → MD → AS (Parentheses, Exponents, Multiply/Divide, Add/Subtract)
  • Multiplication and Division: Do left to right, as they appear
  • Addition and Subtraction: Same, left to right
  • Nested Parentheses: Solve the inner expressions first

Example: 4 + 6 × (2 + 1)² ÷ 3

Step-by-step:

  1. Parentheses: (2 + 1) = 3
  2. Exponents: 3² = 9
  3. Multiplication: 6 × 9 = 54
  4. Division: 54 ÷ 3 = 18
  5. Addition: 4 + 18 = 22

TIP: Always write each step to avoid mistakes!
